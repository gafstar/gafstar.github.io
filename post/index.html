What is Cohort Analysis?

First, let's begin by defining what cohort mean. The word cohort is derived from Latin cohors meaning an enclosure or enclosed group. In digital analytics context it means a group of customers that share something in common. This refers to a groupo of users who have similar characteristics or experiences within a defined time-span.
